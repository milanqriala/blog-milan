Internet Technologies

Computer Internet technology refers to devices, software, hardware and transmission protocols used to connect computers together in order to receive or send data from one computer to another within a small network or as part of a small network within a larger network, such as the Internet. Included are local-area networks (LANs), wide-area networks (WANs), bridging, switching, routing, voice and data integration, wireless integration, dial-up technology, cable access technology, network security and network management.


How technology impacts on our daily life.

So,we see both positive and negative impacts in following reasons.


Positive Impacts:

  • Improved Communication

    • We can communicate with friends, family, and colleagues no matter where they are in the world. Internet technology has introduced new ways of communicating, such as email, instant messaging, and video conferencing.


  • Increased productivity

    • Technology increases the work capacity and reduces the chances of human errors. It helps to keep the corporate in check as it involves powerful computer based system.


  • Education accessibility

    • It ensuring that all students can access computers, tablets, software, and other digital resources. these tools and resources, schools can ensure that all students have the opportunity to learn and succeed.


  • Innovation and Creativity

    • It providing access to information and inspiration, technology can help you generate more ideas and insights for innovation. Leveraging technology for access to information and inspiration is pivotal for fostering creativity and innovation.


  • Research

    • Research makes it easier for students to find information, work together on projects, and handle tasks like data analysis, citations, and virtual experiments, making the research process simpler and more accessible for learning.

Negative Impacts:

  • Health Issues

    • Technology devices can hold a person's attention for long periods. Therefore it causes of dry eyes, double vision, sleep disorder, headaches.


  • Digital addiction

    • It involes the obsessive use of mobile devices, the internet or video games, despite negative consequences to the user of the technology.


  • Privacy concern

    • There are many privacy issue related to information technology, such as electronic surveillance, which involes monitoring people with technology, often without their knowlegde.


  • Cyber bullying

    • It is the use of technology to harass, insult, threaten, and/or intimidate another person. It is increasingly in the spotlight as children and adolescents have easier access than ever to technology and online communnications and engage eith this technology on a daily basis.


  • Sedentary lifestyle

    • It can lead to people shutting themselves in their houses, while engaged in their technology devices. They don't go outside, get any sun or fresh air, and can only communicate through a screen and text.

How technology impacts on business.

So,we see both positive and negative impacts in following reasons.

Positive Impacts:

  • Global Market Access

    • Internet technology helps to business to reach a global audience making it easier for us to broadcast promotions, enabling market expansion beyond geographic boundaries.


  • E-commerece Growth

    • The rise of e-commerece offers business new opportunities for sales, convenience for customers and opportunities for revenue generation.


  • Data-Driven Decision Making

    • Internet technologies enpower businnessess with data collection and data analysis features, insights for informed decision making and strategic planning.


  • Efficient Communication

    • Tools such as email, video conferencing and messaging services promote communication, collaboration and rapid decision making within and outside the organization.


  • Cost Savings

    • Internet technology reduces operational costs through digital communication online marketing and efficient supply chain management resulting in cost savings.

Negative Impacts:

  • Cybersecurity Threats

    • Increasing reliance on the internet can expose business to attacks such as hacking, bata breaches and ransomware attacks.


  • Privacy Concerns

    • The collection and storage of customer data raises privacy concerns, requiring robust measures to strictly protect sensitive information. It sensitive information was not protect strictly then it can lead to legal consequences and damage the trust the customers.


  • Dependency and Downtime

    • Business heavily dependent on internet technology face operational disruptions during internet outages or technical failures, leading to diminished productivity and revenue impact.


  • Increased Competition

    • The access to online markets can increase competition. Businesses can find it challenging to differentiate themselves, especially in saturated markets with low barriers to entry.


  • Onilne Scams

    • When businnesses sell things online, there is a risk of fraud such as fraudulent payments, identify theft and account takeovers. This can cause businesses to lose money and customers may no longer trust them.
